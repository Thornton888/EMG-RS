Changing Price for a Residential Customer:
  1. Search for a customer using service address, name, account name, phone or email.
  2. Click appropriate site and launch Compass to refer to rate options.
  3. Click NEW CASE from Active Site or Active Asset.
  4. Case Record Type = Non-Service
  5. Case Type = SALES
  6. Sub-Type = CHANGE IN PRICE
  7. Status = ACTION REQUIRED
  8. IF SAVE:
    1. No Charge Reason = PRICE INCREASE ROLLBACK
    2. Copy/Paste template into Request Description and fill out:

      New Rate:
      Effective Date:
      Reason:
      Competitor Name:
      Competitor Price:

    3. Click Save
  9. Click CHANGE next to Case Owner
  10. Change to Queue DO NOT CHECK SEND EMAIL
  11. Select Queue = Existing Account Change - Resi (CE)

Submitting a Signed Contract for a Residential customer:
  1. Search for a customer using service address, name, account name, phone or email.
  2. Click appropriate site and launch Compass to refer to rate options.
  3. Click NEW CASE from Active Site or Active Asset./li>
  4. Case Record Type = Non-Service
  5. Case Type = SALES
  6. Sub-Type = CHANGE IN PRICE
  7. Status = ACTION REQUIRED
  8. IF SIGNED CONTRACT:
    1. No Charge Reason = CUSTOMER SAVE / COMPETITIVE RESPONSE
    2. Save
    3. From Case saved screen scroll down to Attachments section and click ATTACH
    4. Find and select the correct signed .pdf document
    5. Save
  9. In the request description write:

    Signed 2 year Contract attached.
    (your name)

  10. Click Save
  11. Click CHANGE next to Case Owner
  12. Change to Queue DO NOT CHECK SEND EMAIL
  13. Select Queue = Existing Account Change - Resi (CE)

Cancelling a Residential Customer:
  1. Search for customer using service address, name, account name, phone or email - and open.
  2. Select the appropriate ACTIVE Asset you're going to close.
  3. Click the CLOSE ASSET button
  4. Enter the close date
    1. Close date is the end of the month. If customer is not going to be at the residence for the entirety of the month credit the remaining difference IF THEY REQUEST IT ONLY
  5. Close Reason = Select most appropriate reason.
  6. Sub Type = RESIDENTIAL
  7. Click GET REMOVAL DATE
  8. Request Description = Why did they call, why are they cancelling etc.
  9. Click Submit

Create Credit for Residential Customer:
  1. Search for customer using service address, name, account name, phone or email - and open.
  2. Select the correct asset from ACTIVE Assets
  3. From Account Details Tab, click RECONCILIATION to view customer's invoices and select the appropriate invoice to see the details.
  4. From the Asset Tab, click the CREATE CREDIT button
  5. Use the Calculator Tool on the tracker to determine the total for the credit
  6. Enter Date
    1. For one-time charges: Charge FROM and TO dates are the the date the charge occured
    2. For regular service charges: Charge FROM and TO dates are the customer's invoice billing cycle.
  7. Select appropriate CORE CHARGE.
  8. Select appropriate Credit Reason
  9. Enter CORE CHARGE AMOUNT
  10. In the Request Description leave a brief explanation of the credit and the amount.
  11. Click SUBMIT - This will also automatically create a case for the Credit.

To be Invoiced (TBI) Credit for Residential Customer:
  1. Search for customer using service address, name, account name, phone or email - and open.
  2. Select the correct asset from ACTIVE Assets
  3. From Account Details Tab, click RECONCILIATION to view customer's invoices and select the appropriate invoice to see the details.
  4. From the Asset Tab, click the CREATE TBI button
  5. Enter Date
    1. For one-time charges: Charge FROM and TO dates are the the date the charge occured
    2. For regular service charges: Charge FROM and TO dates are the customer's invoice billing cycle.
  6. Select appropriate Credit Reason
  7. Enter Credit Amount
  8. Click SUBMIT - This will also automatically create a case for the TBI Credit.
